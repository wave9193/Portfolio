About me

With a strong foundation in Geological Engineering and a deep-rooted passion for technology, I've decided to embrace a new challenge to transition my career to Quality Assurance Engineering and Software Testing. I'm a curious and dedicated person and I have recently joined an Academy in Quality Assurance. Through this experience, I am gaining valuable insights into industry standards, testing methodologies, and software quality management, which by the end of the academy will lead me to pursue the ISTQB certification. While it may seem unconventional to transition from Geological Engineering to Quality Assurance Engineering, I believe that my skills in rigorous analysis, attention to detail, thorough planning, and data interpretation will prove invaluable in ensuring the reliability and efficiency of software systems. More than anything, I approach this new career path with humility and a willingness to adapt. I understand that there will be a learning curve, but I am excited to tackle challenges head-on and grow both personally and professionally in the process.
